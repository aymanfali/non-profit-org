About Us

Our history

  • UNESCO was created to decide what kind of society we wanted to build together after the destruction of two World Wars.
  • As early as 1942, global leaders began imagining an organization that would use education, culture, science and information to build lasting peace.
  • Over the years, UNESCO has implemented initiatives that have radically improved lives around the world, and shaped global action in its field of competence, through nation-wide educational and scientific programmes, the creation of tsunami warning systems, and the idea of World Heritage. Today, we are continuing this work.

Our Values

Our #SharingHumanity campaign reminds us of our global values, culture, and knowledge. By strengthening these ties, we build the foundations of peace between people and nations.
